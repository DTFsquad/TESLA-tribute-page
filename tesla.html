Nikola Tesla

The image of Nicola Tesla from Facebook group Nikola Tesla Explorers
  1. Early life
    • Tesla was the fourth of five children.
    • In 1861, Tesla attended primary school.
    • In 1870, Tesla moved far north to attend high school. He finished a four-year term in three years, graduating in 1873
    • In 1875, Tesla enrolled at Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria, on a Military Frontier scholarship
    • In December 1878, Tesla left Graz and severed all relations with his family to hide the fact that he dropped out of school.
  2. Working at Edison
    • In 1882, Tesla got a job in Paris with the Continental Edison Company.
    • In 1884, Teslacis being moved to Edison Machine Works, a manufacturing division situated in New York City.
    • Tesla had been working at the Machine Works for a total of six months when he quit.
